2. Duration

📌 Special Note — Rolling Average (Campaign Construct, Section 5) Rolling Average calculates the average volume over a rolling window of X days. The total campaign duration (End Date minus Start Date) must be an exact multiple of X to ensure all rolling cycles complete fully. Example: a 7-day rolling window requires a campaign duration of 7, 14, 21, or 28 days. Adjust either the rolling window or the campaign duration to satisfy this rule.
